About


Hi, I'm Larry.

Born in Philly, raised in Indiana, and now I live in Brooklyn with my beautiful wife, Erin (married on leap day of 2012), our dog and cat, and three of our best friends. I graduated from Indiana University in 2011 with a degree in journalism and a concentration in fine arts. I'm a full-time freelancer working on the web and in print for a number of different clients.

If you're in the NYC area, I'd love to grab coffee or a beer.

I randomly made Mashable the other day with a tumblr about how Mitt Romney's logo looks like toothpaste.
